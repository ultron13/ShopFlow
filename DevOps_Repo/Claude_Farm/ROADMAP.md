# FarmConnect SA — Product Roadmap

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** May 2026

The roadmap is anchored to business milestones, not feature lists. We only move to the next phase when the current one proves its unit economics.

---

## Q2 2026 — Corridor MVP (Months 1-3)

**North Star:** One corridor works end-to-end. First paying restaurant. First farmer paid in 48 hours.

### Platform
- [x] Core data models (Cooperative, Listing, Order, Collection, Delivery, Payment)
- [ ] Buyer registration and listing browse
- [ ] Order placement with Ozow payment
- [ ] Field agent grading form (offline-capable)
- [ ] Ops admin dispatch and payment queue
- [ ] Farmer payout via Stitch (48-hour SLA)
- [ ] WhatsApp notifications (order confirmed, dispatched, paid)
- [ ] Auto-generated VAT-compliant invoice (PDF)
- [ ] Quality dispute flow

### Operations
- [ ] Tzaneen field agent hired and trained
- [ ] 3 cooperatives onboarded (ZZ2, Mahela Fresh, Limpopo Tomato Growers)
- [ ] Contracted refrigerated transport on N1 (1 route, 5x/week)
- [ ] Gauteng micro-hub (cold room access arranged)
- [ ] 15-20 Sandton/Rosebank restaurants onboarded

### Milestone Gate
- First week: 1 restaurant, 1 delivery, 1 farmer paid
- Month 3: 20+ restaurants ordering weekly, farmer SLA met 100%

## Q3 2026 — B2B Scale (Months 4-6)

**North Star:** Corridor breakeven. 25-30 restaurants ordering weekly. 54,400 kg/month.

### Platform
- [ ] Standing orders (recurring weekly orders for buyers)
- [ ] Demand forecast view (farmers see aggregate weekly demand)
- [ ] Price comparison widget (vs. current market prices)
- [ ] Route optimisation assist (Mapbox-powered clustering)
- [ ] Buyer referral programme
- [ ] SMS fallback for farmers without WhatsApp
- [ ] Weekly GMV and unit economics dashboard (internal)

### Operations
- [ ] 5+ cooperatives active
- [ ] 50-80 restaurants
- [ ] Second logistics route (peak demand)

### Milestone Gate
- Contribution margin per kg > R1.00 for 4 consecutive weeks
- Zero payment SLA breaches
- NPS from buyers > 30

- [ ] Multi-product orders (single order, multiple produce lines)

### Platform

- [ ] B2G procurement portal (full government buyer experience)

## Year 3 — Path to Breakeven

**Target:** R500M-1.5B GMV | Operating breakeven at ~R1B GMV

### Platform
- [ ] B2C module (consumer aggregated delivery)
- [ ] Predictive inventory recommendations
- [ ] Logistics network owned-vehicle economics (if volumes justify)
- [ ] Farmer financial services (credit scoring from payment history)

### Business
- [ ] Multi-province operations
- [ ] National government contract (school nutrition or hospitals)
- [ ] DFI co-investment secured

---

## What We Will NOT Build (Yet)

These are explicitly out of scope until the corridor proves itself:

- Consumer-facing app (B2C until Year 3)
- Farmer credit/lending product (requires NCA licence)
- Drone/satellite farm monitoring
- Commodity trading / futures (regulatory complexity)
- Own trucking fleet (too capital-intensive pre-Series A)
- International expansion (premature)
